FLAPPY BIRD

Play Flappy Bird
Flappy Bird is an arcade-style game in which the player controls the bird Faby, which moves persistently to the right. The player is tasked with navigating Faby through pairs of pipes that have equally sized gaps placed at random heights.

Creator: Dong Nguyen
Original Release: 2013

Instructions

  1. Use Left Mouse Click to move the bird slightly upwards.
  2. The bird will continue to fall down.
  3. Each successful pass through a pair of pipes awards you one point.
  4. Colliding with a pipe or falling on the ground ends the game.
  5. Have Fun!
