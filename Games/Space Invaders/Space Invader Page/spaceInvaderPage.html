SPACE INVADERS

Play Space Invaders
Space Invaders is a fixed shooter in which the player moves a laser cannon horizontally across the bottom of the screen and fires at aliens overhead. The aliens begin to move left and right as a group, shifting downward every few seconds.

Creator: Tomohiro Nishikado
Original Release: 1978

Instructions

  1. Use Left Arrow Key and Right Arrow Key to move player in the respective direction.
  2. Use Space Bar to fire laser at the aliens above you.
  3. The aliens keep getting lower every few seconds as well as shoot laser at you.
  4. If the laser touches an alien, it gets destroyed and you are awarded a point for it.
  5. If the aliens reach at your altitude, or you get hit by alien laser the game shall be over.
  6. Have Fun!
